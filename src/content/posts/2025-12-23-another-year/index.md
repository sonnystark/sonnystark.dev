## A 2025 summary... of sorts

I had some really grand ideas and ambitions for this post. As it turns out, time had other plans.  
I wanted to do a proper "Best of 2025" post where I'd guide you through my favourite albums of the year and what podcasts really dominated the airwaves. Alas, here we are without any of them. It's a little bit like showing up to a friends birthday party without bringing a gift. But that's life sometimes. As I am writing this, though, I realise that I still have another week or so to correct this so that's what I will aim for. I will leave you with that and get right back to my best-of-lists and all the other stuff I had planned for this post. Pretend that this post never happened.
